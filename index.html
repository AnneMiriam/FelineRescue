Colorado Feline Rescue

Welcome to Colorado Feline Rescue. We are a non-profit that serves the Front Range from Colorado Springs to Fort Collins. We rescue any cats that are in need of a home. All cats have been given full health checks, spayed/neutered, and fully vaccinated(depending on age), before they are up for adoption. Our kitties, also, go through a behavioral review to make sure they find the best fit for their forever home.

We also offer homing for working cats. Working cats are kitties who don't like being around people very much and would prefer a home in a barn or shop where they can explore and help you keep the rodent population down. We do require those looking for a working cat still be responsible for the cats care: two meals a day, access to fresh water at all times, as well as, annual Vet visits and medical care as needed.
